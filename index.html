About

Club Kerala is an Indian professional football club based in Malapuram district of Kerala. Founded in 2017,the club competes in the I-league,the co-existing primary tier of Indian football. Club Kerala plays a major role in moulding young talents of Kerala. Thousands of young players are getting trained under this club.

Club Kerala Young Talent Search

Club Kerala Young Talent Search is an initiative started in 2019 under Club Kerala Foundation.It is started with an intention of enriching the young talents of Kerala. Under this initiative we are training young talented girls and boys to have a good career in football field. Every year we organise a selection trial in Malapuram.Selected candidates will be trained under Club Kerala Foundation.
